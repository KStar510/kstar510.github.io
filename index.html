About me

Hello and welcome! I am a recent graduate from the University of California - Irvine with a B.S. in Game Design and Interactive Media (GDIM) as well as minors in Informatics and Computer Science (ICS) and Film & Media Studies.

I have been a lifelong gamer, particularly a fan of Nintendo games, and I hope to one day utilize my skills that I have learned in game design and programming to create fun and wonderful things that people can enjoy!

Over the past 4+ years, I have a wide range of experiences from making various small games, having worked on various different game projects, including some work for use on mobile devices. While studying game design, I have learned a great amount about the development processs including researching, prototyping, and playtesting features.

I have a well-rounded background in design and programming, creating a wide variety of different features, including various UI/UX interfaces, AI for both friends and foes, various RPG elements, and even online multiplayer.

If you have any questions about my work, feel free to reach out!
